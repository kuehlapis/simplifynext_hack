Review of Your Rental Agreement

Dear Recipient,

I hope this email finds you well.

I am writing to bring to your attention several clauses in your rental agreement that may be considered high-risk. It is important to understand these clauses and their potential implications before signing the agreement.

Here is a summary of the problematic clauses and my recommendations:

1. Clause: The Tenant shall pay rent in the amount of SGD 500.00 per month, but the Landlord reserves the right to increase rent by any amount at any time without prior notice.
Recommendation: Negotiate for a reasonable rent increase clause.

2. Clause: A security deposit of SGD 2000.00 shall be paid, which will not be refunded under any circumstances, including but not limited to the Tenant never actually moving in.
Recommendation: Negotiate to reduce deposit to 2 months maximum and ensure refund terms.

3. Clause: Tenant agrees to allow the Landlord unrestricted access to the premises at all hours, including weekends, public holidays, and during the Tenant's sleep.
Recommendation: Ensure entry notice clause is present and reasonable.

4. Clause: The Tenant waives all legal rights, including the right to complain, negotiate, or seek fairness. All disputes shall be resolved exclusively by the Landlord's personal opinion.
Recommendation: This clause is illegal and should be removed.

5. Clause: The Agreement shall last for 99 years or until the Tenant's spirit is broken, whichever comes first.
Recommendation: Ensure tenancy period meets minimum legal requirements.

6. Clause: Governing Law: This Agreement shall be governed by the Laws of Singapore, as well as the arbitrary mood swings of the Landlord.
Recommendation: This is not a valid governing law clause and should be removed.

7. Clause: Late payment of rent shall incur a penalty equal to the GDP of a small nation, payable immediately.
Recommendation: Negotiate for a reasonable late payment penalty.

8. Clause: Tenant must perform weekly entertainment (karaoke, interpretive dance, or comedy routines) for the Landlord without compensation.
Recommendation: This clause is unreasonable and should be removed.

9. Clause: Any noise complaints made by the Tenant will be considered a violation of this Agreement and grounds for eviction.
Recommendation: This clause is unreasonable and should be removed.

10. Clause: The Landlord reserves the right to use Tenant's room for storage of personal items, livestock, or experimental technology.
Recommendation: This clause is illegal and should be removed.

11. Clause: Tenant shall provide free labor for all landlord's projects, including but not limited to gardening, laundry, and emotional support.
Recommendation: This clause is unreasonable and should be removed.

12. Clause: The Tenant must ask permission before breathing loudly within the unit.
Recommendation: This clause is unreasonable and should be removed.

13. Clause: Any guest visiting the Tenant must pay an entrance fee to the Landlord, amount to be determined daily.
Recommendation: This clause is unreasonable and should be removed.

14. Clause: Tenant must maintain the apartment temperature strictly within Landlord's comfort range, regardless of Tenant's survival needs.
Recommendation: This clause is unreasonable and should be removed.

15. Clause: If the Tenant leaves behind any belongings, they automatically become property of the Landlord, including sentimental items.
Recommendation: This clause is illegal and should be removed.

16. Clause: Tenant must display unwavering gratitude towards the Landlord at all times. Failure to smile in Landlord's presence constitutes breach.
Recommendation: This clause is unreasonable and should be removed.

17. Clause: The Tenant may not question the fairness of any clause in this Agreement, verbally or in writing.
Recommendation: This clause is illegal and should be removed.

18. Clause: The Tenant agrees to pay additional 'Goodwill Rent' whenever the Landlord feels underappreciated.
Recommendation: This clause is unreasonable and should be removed.

19. Clause: The Landlord is entitled to 50% of all Tenant's lottery winnings, inheritances, or found coins on the street.
Recommendation: This clause is illegal and should be removed.

20. Clause: Any attempt by the Tenant to move out requires a six-year written notice.
Recommendation: Negotiate for reasonable notice period.

21. Clause: Final Clause: The Landlord reserves the right to add, remove, or modify any clause in this Agreement at any time without Tenant's knowledge or consent.
Recommendation: This clause is illegal and should be removed.
